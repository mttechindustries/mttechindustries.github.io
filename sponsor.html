Sponsorship paths

Support can be public, private, or project-specific.

Open development sponsor

Supports selected public repositories, documentation, maintenance, packaging, and releases. Sponsor recognition can be public when requested.

Sponsored development

Funds a defined feature, integration, demonstration, validation package, or domain-specific implementation under written scope.

Equipment or infrastructure sponsor

Provides hardware, hosting, software services, data access, manufacturing support, or other resources needed to complete a defined lane.

Commercial partner

Begins with controlled review and can proceed into pilot, license, investment, acquisition, or strategic-development discussions.
