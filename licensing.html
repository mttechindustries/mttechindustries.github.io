How It Works

Three Licensing Structures

Field-of-Use License

License a specific patent for use within a defined market, application, or product category. You get the IP, MT Tech retains ownership and can license in non-competing fields.

Best for: companies that want exclusive rights in their vertical without full acquisition

Portfolio License

License multiple or all patent lanes as a bundle. Full portfolio access with MT Tech as the continuing R&D partner producing new IP in adjacent areas.

Best for: platforms that want comprehensive coverage across the full Omnimathematics stack

Research Partnership

Sponsored development of domain-specific implementations — AI safety, robotics, vet tech, or other verticals. MT Tech builds and delivers, you own the applied output.

Best for: R&D teams that need custom implementation rather than raw IP
