Investment Thesis

Fund the review path, not public sprawl.

The portfolio should be evaluated as a controlled commercial stack: private systems for demo/licensing/pilot review, a verified filed IP backbone, a near-product patent-filed lead in WoofWizeX-FRS, and controlled unfiled advanced R&D that stays private unless a serious review path exists.

Capital can be applied toward patent prosecution support, controlled demo packaging, pilot packets, claim charts, diligence materials, source evidence organization, and partner-specific technical review — without exposing implementation-sensitive material publicly.

This is an IP, product-system, and licensing story. It is not a generic SaaS metrics story and not a pile of unrelated project names.
