Field-Respectful Technology

Wi-Fi CSI Analysis for Biological Signal Extraction

WoofWizeX-FRS leverages Channel State Information (CSI) from standard Wi-Fi signals to detect and analyze biological patterns, emotional states, and behavioral indicators in animals without any invasive monitoring.

Privacy-First Design Philosophy

No cameras, no microphones, no GPS tracking, no mandatory wearables. Complete respect for animal privacy and natural behavior.
