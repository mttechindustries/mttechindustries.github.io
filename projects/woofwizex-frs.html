The Problem

Animals Cannot Report How They Feel

Animal health and welfare monitoring relies almost entirely on observable behavioral changes — changes that are often late indicators. Continuous monitoring requires invasive hardware, restraint, or visual surveillance that alters natural behavior.

WoofWizeX-FRS solves this without any of those constraints. The sensing mechanism respects the animal's environment completely while delivering continuous, real-time intelligence about biological and behavioral state.

What Existing Approaches Miss
Wearables that alter natural behavior by their presence
Cameras that compromise privacy and create observer effect
Periodic check-ins that miss critical early-warning signals
Invasive monitoring that requires physical access and restraint
What This Enables

Continuous Monitoring With Zero Footprint

Real-time behavioral intelligence that does not disturb the subject, modify the environment, or require new infrastructure.

Health Monitoring

Continuous health signal extraction and early-warning anomaly detection without physical contact or clinical intervention.

Behavioral State Classification

Emotional state, stress indicators, activity levels, and behavioral pattern analysis — in real time, without observer effect.

Privacy-First Architecture

No recording, no visual surveillance, no location data. Privacy is structural — built into how the system works, not how it's configured.
