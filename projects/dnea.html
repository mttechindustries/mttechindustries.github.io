DNEA

Directional Noise-Embedded Audio

A proprietary framework for embedding, encoding, and delivering audio content within directional noise fields. Combines ultrasonic parametric delivery, infrasound entrainment, and psychoacoustic masking for precision spatial audio targeting without perceptible signal presence.

Core Technology

Parametric Directional Audio Embedding

DNEA enables audio content to be embedded within ambient noise and delivered with spatial precision to defined target zones — without the content being perceptible outside that zone or detectable as conventional audio signal. The system exploits the physics of ultrasonic heterodyning and infrasound interference to create audio that only coherently decodes within a defined acoustic radius.

Delivery Mechanisms

  • Ultrasonic heterodyning (parametric speakers)
  • Phased transducer arrays for spatial targeting
  • Infrasound carrier layers (10–19 Hz)
  • Binaural beat embedding (theta/gamma frequency pairing)
  • Sparse spectral masking within ambient noise

Signal Architecture

  • Phase-inverted subliminal encoding
  • Time-stretched syllable delivery
  • Theta-gamma coupling (4–8 Hz / 40 Hz)
  • Real-time biometric adaptive feedback
  • Error-correction for signal integrity under noise

Frequency Layer Architecture

Infrasound Layer (10–19 Hz)

Physiological priming — slowed heart rate, cortisol reduction, alpha/theta brainwave entrainment via interference patterns.

Theta Band (4–8 Hz)

Memory consolidation targeting. Audio cues delivered in 0.5–1 second bursts synced to detected theta peaks during NREM sleep stages.

Gamma Overlay (40 Hz)

Synaptic plasticity enhancement. Synchronized auditory pulses overlaid on theta peaks to amplify retention response.

Ultrasonic Carrier (>20 kHz)

Heterodyned parametric delivery — signal only becomes audible as the ultrasonic carriers interact within the target zone.

Applications

Cognitive Enhancement — EasyZz LinguaFlow

DNEA serves as the delivery backbone of the EasyZz sleep-learning platform. Content is embedded into sleep-safe ambient audio and delivered during detected NREM phases via under-mattress transducers or acoustic pillow arrays, bypassing conscious awareness while engaging memory consolidation processes.

Sleep-Phase Delivery

Phase-locked audio cues delivered to sleeping subjects during verified theta windows. Vocabulary, patterns, and structured knowledge reinforced without waking.

Biometric Adaptation

Real-time EEG and heart rate feedback adjusts audio intensity and timing, maximizing retention while preventing sleep disruption.

Wellness & Spatial Audio Targeting

Positive DNEA applies the same directional embedding framework to ambient wellness delivery — calming affirmations and psychoacoustic content embedded in pink noise and beamed to precise spatial zones (desk, bed, workspace). Coherent decoding only within a 2-meter target radius.

Precision Zone Delivery

Audio only coherently resolves within defined acoustic target zones. Outside those zones, only ambient noise is present — no perceptible signal leakage.

Consent-Based Operation

System architecture includes transparency mode and opt-in consent layers to ensure ethical deployment in wellness and enterprise contexts.
