The Problem

Rules Can Be Optimized Around. Mathematics Cannot.

An AI system that is sufficiently capable can learn to satisfy the letter of a rule while violating its intent — because the optimization landscape itself has not changed. The constraint is external. OURS addresses this by operating at the level of the objective function itself — making non-compliant paths structurally expensive through mathematical landscape restructuring, not post-hoc enforcement.
