About MT Tech Industries

MT Tech Industries LLC is an independent research and development organization based in Michigan, focused on advancing fundamental understanding in applied mathematics, computational physics, and system design.

Our work emphasizes rigorous mathematical foundations, systematic validation, and transparent documentation of research progress.

Research Philosophy

  • Open documentation of research methodologies and findings
  • Emphasis on reproducibility and verification
  • Commitment to intellectual property transparency and prior art establishment
