Penalty-Compliance Framework

Mathematical enforcement of AI compliance and truthfulness. Replaces brittle heuristic constraints with penalty-augmented objective functions that make non-compliant behavior pathologically expensive for optimization.

Core Innovation: Compliance as gradient of least resistance. Non-truthful paths become mathematical dead-ends.
Mechanism: Predictive violation detection + automatic repair in abstract domain before physical manifestation.
